How to register at EasyShop.ph

  1. Visit https://EasyShop.ph.
  2. Click the Register button located at the right top most part of the page (Gray button) and complete the following details:

Complete mechanics

EasyShop.ph, in coordination with Nelsoft Technology Inc., will hold a discount promotion on their website and will be promoted through social media. In a span of 48 hours, there will be 58 items for sale (only one stock per item), which prices will drop by 2% per hour until it reaches 99% or until SOLD. A buyer will have to race against time and other buyers for him/her to be able to purchase the item they want, at the best price possible. The buyers can only use Credit Card (VISA and Mastercard) and PayPal to pay for the item that they will purchase from this promo.

Step 1. Buyer should register at https://EasyShop.ph and complete the following:

  • Username (The username must be 5-25 characters long.)
  • Password (Password must be alphanumeric with a minimum of 6 characters.)
  • Confirm Password
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number

The buyer should also “Like” EasyShop.ph on Facebook (https://www.facebook.com/EasyShopPhilippines).

Step 2. Buyer should visit the homepage https://EasyShop.ph/home to check the items included in the sale.

Step 3. The prices of the items included in the promotion are decreasing by 2% every hour. There is only ONE (1) stock per item, and only ONE item can be purchased per account. The buyer will have to choose ONE item from the list of items that are included in the promotion from EasyShop.ph’s page. The buyer will be redirected to the product’s page.

Step 4. The buyer will have to click “Buy Now” button.

Step 5. The buyer will be redirected to a page to review his Shopping Cart. The buyer needs to review the following: Item name, Color, Price, Quantity, Sub Total). If all the details are correct, the buyer has to click “Proceed to Payment” button. Otherwise, he can click “Continue Shopping” if he plans to add other products, which are not included with the said promotion, to his Shopping Cart.

Step 6. After clicking the “Proceed to Payment” button, the buyer will now be redirected to the Payment Review page where he needs to review his Shipping Details: Name, Full Address, City, Country, Mobile, Telephone Number).

Step 7. The buyer will need to choose between the payment options available on the same page. For this promotion, the buyer can only choose between Credit or Debit Card and PayPal for mode of payment.

Step 8. The buyer has to acknowledge that they have read and understood EasyShop.ph’s Privacy Policy and will have to agree with it by ticking the box beside it.

Step 9. They buyer has to click the “Proceed to Payment” button. He will be redirected to the system of the mode of payment he selected to complete the payment. (If he selected Credit or Debit Card option, he will be redirected to its system. If he selected PayPal, he will be redirected to PayPal’s system.)

Step 10. The buyer will be notified that the payment transaction succeeded through Payment Result page which includes the following:

Transaction Details (Invoice Number, Total Amount, Date Added) and Purchased Product Details (Product, Name of Seller, Price, Quantity, Subtotal, Other Fee and Total Fee) A confirmation email and text message will also be sent to the buyer.

Step 11. The payment will be verified from EasyShop.ph’s end. Once confirmed, the item will be prepared for shipping, and the buyer will just have to wait for the item to be delivered to the address that they have entered in the system. The shipping cost will be shouldered by Nelsoft Technology Inc.

Step 12. Once the item is received, the buyer will need to click the “Item Received” button on his/her user account dashboard. Should problem/defect arise with the item, the buyer will have to notify EasyShop.ph for replacement within 15 days. Otherwise, the payment will be automatically remitted to the seller (Nelsoft Technology Inc.), whether the item’s receipt is confirmed or not.

Every item that has been sold will be removed from the site.

Criteria, disqualification and other rules

  • Must have completed the registration form in the site and have confirmed account his account as well.
  • Once the account is confirmed, the buyer should “Like” EasyShop.ph on Facebook.
  • Buyer may not purchase more than one (1) product.
  • Nelsoft Technology Inc. will shoulder the shipping of the product to the buyer’s address.
  • The Countdown Sale is open to all who live and temporarily stays in the Philippines. The promotion is void where prohibited by law.
